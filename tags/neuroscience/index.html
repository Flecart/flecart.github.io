What is a neural system? A neural system is an intricately organized network of specialized cellsprimarily neurons, along with a variety of supportive glial cellsthat processes and transmits information via electrical and chemical signals. In biological organisms, such systems underpin the entire nervous system, coordinating functions that range from basic reflexes to the complex interplay of perception, thought, and behavior.
